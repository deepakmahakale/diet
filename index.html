📋 General Guidelines

  • Include small meals at regular intervals and follow meal timings
  • Keep a 3-4 hour gap between each meal
  • Avoid skipping and overeating at one time
  • Follow the portion size of your meals as mentioned
  • Keep your calories intact
  • Avoid heavy and late meals at dinner time
  • Include variety in the diet by including seasonal fruits and vegetables
  • Avoid fruits along with meals - eat fruit as a separate meal
  • Focus on food and chew slowly, ensuring good digestion
  • Adopt healthier cooking practices: grilling, steaming, sautéing, broiling, roasting
  • Avoid screens while having food
  • Follow fluid and salt restrictions as per cardiologist's recommendation
